Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Imagely

What is Imagely?

Imagely is an AI-powered platform that allows users to generate, edit, and enhance images with ease. Whether you need to create new visuals from scratch or fine-tune existing photos, Imagely provides the tools to achieve high-quality results.

How does Imagely generate images using AI?

Imagely utilizes advanced AI algorithms trained on vast datasets to generate images based on user inputs. You can describe the type of image you want, and the AI will create a custom visual that matches your specifications.

Can I edit existing images on Imagely?

Absolutely! Imagely offers a suite of editing tools that allow you to modify colors, add effects, adjust lighting, remove backgrounds, and more. Whether you're starting with an AI-generated image or uploading your own, you can refine it to perfection.

Is there a limit to how many images I can generate or edit?

Imagely offers various subscription plans that cater to different needs. While free users may have limited access to image generation and editing features, premium plans provide more extensive usage allowances and additional features.

How secure is my data on Imagely?

We prioritize your privacy and the security of your data. All images, including those generated and edited, are stored securely, and you have full control over your content. Imagely does not share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
